What is this?

This is a live chess board that lets anyone come and play as many moves as they'd like. It's inherently not very competitive, but that's okay. Sometimes you just want to come and play a couple moves and sometimes you'll want to battle it out with multiple people playing each side. It's a little bit like playing chess at the park. Just remember to be nice, these are real people you're playing against.
